# Marketing Campaign Dashboard 📊

An end-to-end marketing analytics project analyzing customer behavior, 
campaign performance, and spending patterns using Python.

## 📊 Dataset
- 2,240 customers from a real marketing campaign dataset
- Source: Kaggle — Marketing Campaign by rodsaldanha

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
- Which customer segments spend the most?
- Which campaigns had the best response rates?
- What factors predict customer spending?

## 🔍 Key Findings
- **Strong income-spend correlation (0.79)** — higher income customers 
  spend significantly more
- **Campaign 4 had the highest response rate** among all 5 campaigns
- **Higher educated customers** tend to spend more on average
- **Income is the strongest predictor** of total spend

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Dataset: Kaggle Marketing Campaign

## 📁 Files
- `Marketing campaign analysis.ipynb` — full analysis notebook
- `marketing_campaign.csv` — dataset

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Prioritize high income segments for premium campaigns
- Replicate Campaign 4 strategy for future campaigns
- Use education level and income as key targeting variables
